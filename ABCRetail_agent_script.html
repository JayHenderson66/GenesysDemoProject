Pre-Call Checklist
Confirm customer identity (name, account number)
Review open orders and any active exceptions
Check for open cases — note case ID and current stage
Review journey timeline — note last interaction and sentiment
Identify primary reason for call
Check NPS score — if low or at-risk, flag for retention

Order Status Scripts
Order In Transit
"I can see your order [PO#] is currently in transit with [Carrier]. The last tracking event shows [last event] as of [date]. Your estimated delivery date is [ETA]. Would you like me to send the tracking link to your email?"
Order Delayed
"I can see there's been a delay on order [PO#]. [Delay reason]. We sincerely apologize for this inconvenience. The updated estimated delivery is [new ETA]. I'm going to escalate this to our logistics team to get you a more precise update within 24 hours."
Order On Hold
"I see order [PO#] is currently on hold. [Hold reason]. I'm transferring you to our accounts team who can resolve this right away. Your hold reference is [Case ID]."

Standard Close
"Is there anything else I can help you with today? … Thank you for choosing ABC Retail. We appreciate your business. Have a great day!"
Post-Issue Close
"I understand this has been frustrating, and I want to assure you we're committed to resolving this. You'll receive a case number by SMS shortly. Our team will follow up within [SLA timeframe]. Thank you for your patience."
