Category 1 — Shipping & Delivery

1.1 — What are your standard delivery timeframes?

Standard delivery timeframes vary by product type and location:

  • In-stock items: 5–7 business days for standard delivery; 2–3 business days for expedited delivery.
  • Made-to-order items: 4–8 weeks depending on the item. Your order confirmation email will include an estimated delivery date.
  • Large/oversized items (furniture, rugs, mattresses): 7–14 business days. White glove delivery is available for select items.

Delivery times may be extended during peak periods (holidays, sale events). You will receive a shipping confirmation email with tracking information once your order has been dispatched.

1.2 — Do you offer free shipping?

Yes. ABC Retail offers free standard shipping on all orders over $150 within the contiguous United States. For orders under $150, a flat shipping fee of $9.95 applies.

Exceptions:

  • Oversized items (furniture, large rugs, mattresses) are subject to a delivery surcharge, displayed at checkout.
  • White glove delivery is a premium service available for an additional fee starting at $79.
  • Orders shipping to Alaska, Hawaii, and US territories do not qualify for free standard shipping.

1.3 — Can I change my delivery address after placing an order?

You can request a delivery address change within 2 hours of placing your order by contacting our support team. After that window, your order may already be in processing and address changes cannot be guaranteed.

To request a change:

  1. Call us at 1-800-ABC-HOME (available 7am–9pm PT, 7 days a week)
  2. Have your order number ready
  3. Provide the corrected delivery address

If your order has already shipped, you will need to contact the carrier directly. We can provide the carrier and tracking number.

1.4 — What is white glove delivery and how do I request it?

White glove delivery is a premium service available for large furniture and select items. It includes:

  • Delivery to your room of choice (not just curbside or doorstep)
  • Unpacking and removal of all packaging materials
  • Basic assembly if required
  • Removal of your old item (available in select areas for an additional fee)

White glove delivery can be selected at checkout for eligible items. If you missed the option at checkout, contact our support team within 24 hours of placing your order. White glove delivery is not available in all areas — zip code eligibility is confirmed at checkout.

1.5 — My shipment is delayed — what should I do?

We apologize for the inconvenience. Delays can occur due to carrier issues, weather events, or high order volumes.

Steps to take:

  1. Check your tracking link (included in your shipping confirmation email) for the latest carrier update.
  2. If your tracking shows no movement for more than 3 business days, contact our support team.
  3. If your estimated delivery date has passed by more than 2 business days, you are eligible to request an expedited re-shipment or a full refund.

Our team can open a carrier investigation on your behalf. Resolution typically takes 3–5 business days. For urgent situations, please call us directly at 1-800-ABC-HOME.

Category 2 — Returns & Exchanges

2.1 — What is your return policy?

ABC Retail offers a 30-day return policy on most items from the date of delivery.

Eligibility requirements:

  • Item must be in original, unused condition
  • Original packaging must be intact where possible
  • Proof of purchase (order number or receipt) required

Non-returnable items:

  • Made-to-order and custom items
  • Mattresses (once removed from packaging)
  • Final sale items (clearly marked at time of purchase)
  • Items damaged due to customer misuse

2.2 — How do I initiate a return?

You can initiate a return in three ways:

Online:

  1. Log in to your ABC Retail account
  2. Go to Order History and select the order
  3. Click “Return Item” and follow the prompts
  4. Print your prepaid return shipping label

By phone: Call 1-800-ABC-HOME (7am–9pm PT, 7 days a week).

In-store: Returns can be made at any ABC Retail location. Bring your item and proof of purchase.

Once your return is received and inspected, a refund will be issued within 5–7 business days to your original payment method.

2.3 — How do I exchange an item?

ABC Retail handles exchanges as a return plus a new order.

  1. Initiate a return for the original item (see Article 2.2)
  2. Place a new order for the replacement item

If the replacement item is the same price or lower, no additional charge applies once the return is processed. If it is higher, you will be charged the difference.

For exchanges due to a defect or shipping damage, we will cover the cost of return shipping and expedite the replacement at no charge.

2.4 — I received a damaged item — what do I do?

We sincerely apologize. Please follow these steps:

  1. Document the damage: Take clear photos of the item and packaging before moving or unpacking further.
  2. Contact us within 48 hours of delivery — this is required for damage claims.
  3. Do not discard the packaging — it may be needed for the carrier claim.

You can report damage by calling 1-800-ABC-HOME, using live chat, or emailing support@abcretail.com with your order number and photos. We will arrange a replacement or full refund at your preference. Return shipping for damaged items is always free.

Category 3 — Order Status & Tracking

3.1 — How do I check the status of my order?

You can check your order status in several ways:

Online (fastest): Log in to your ABC Retail account → Order History → select your order.

Email: Your shipping confirmation email contains a real-time tracking link.

Phone: Call 1-800-ABC-HOME with your order number.

Order statuses:

  • Processing: Order received, payment confirmed, preparing for shipment
  • Shipped: Order dispatched; tracking link is active
  • Out for Delivery: With the carrier for delivery today
  • Delivered: Carrier has confirmed delivery
  • Delayed: A hold has been placed — see tracking for details

3.2 — My tracking number isn’t working — what should I do?

Tracking numbers can take up to 24 hours to activate after your shipping confirmation email is sent. This is normal.

If your tracking link is still not showing results after 24 hours:

  1. Copy the tracking number and enter it directly on the carrier’s website (UPS, FedEx, or USPS)
  2. If still inactive, contact our support team — we will verify the shipment status

If your order shows “Shipped” in your ABC Retail account, your package is on its way.

3.3 — Can I cancel my order?

Orders can be cancelled within 2 hours of placement if they have not yet entered processing.

To cancel: call 1-800-ABC-HOME immediately or use live chat on the website.

If your order has already been processed or shipped, cancellation is no longer possible. You can refuse the delivery and a return will be initiated automatically, or return the item after delivery.

Made-to-order items cannot be cancelled once production has begun (typically within 24 hours of order placement).

3.4 — My order says “Delivered” but I haven’t received it — what do I do?

  1. Check around your delivery location — packages are sometimes left at a side door, garage, or with a neighbor.
  2. Wait 24 hours — carriers occasionally mark a package as delivered before it physically arrives.
  3. Check with household members — someone else may have brought it inside.
  4. Contact us if the package still hasn’t arrived after 24 hours.

We will open a carrier investigation. If the package is confirmed lost, we will send a replacement or issue a full refund within 5 business days of the investigation closing.

3.5 — Can I modify my order after it’s been placed?

Order modifications are possible within 2 hours of placement before the order enters processing.

What can be changed: delivery address, item color or size (subject to availability), adding or removing items.

What cannot be changed: payment method; items in a made-to-order or custom order once confirmed.

To request a modification, call 1-800-ABC-HOME as soon as possible with your order number ready.

4.2 — How do I care for upholstered furniture?

Daily care: Fluff and rotate cushions weekly. Vacuum fabric surfaces monthly using a low-suction upholstery attachment.

Stain treatment: Blot (do not rub) spills immediately. Use a mild fabric cleaner appropriate for your fabric type. Always test on a hidden area first.

Fabric codes (found on the care tag):

  • W — Water-based cleaner only
  • S — Dry clean solvent only; no water
  • WS — Either water-based or solvent cleaner
  • X — Vacuum only; no liquid cleaners

Professional cleaning is recommended every 1–2 years for heavily used pieces.

4.3 — How do I care for wood furniture?

Routine care: Dust regularly with a soft, dry or slightly damp cloth. Avoid silicone-based polishes. Use a furniture wax or polish appropriate for your finish type.

Protection: Use coasters, placemats, and trivets to prevent heat and moisture damage. Keep furniture out of direct sunlight. Maintain indoor humidity between 35–55%.

Minor scratches: Light scratches can often be buffed out with a matching wood marker or touch-up crayon. Deeper damage should be assessed by a professional restorer.

4.4 — How do I care for rugs?

Routine care: Vacuum regularly (2–3 times per week in high-traffic areas). Rotate your rug 180° every 6–12 months for even wear. Use a rug pad to prevent slipping.

Spills: Blot immediately — never rub. For liquid spills, use cold water and a small amount of mild dish soap. For solid spills, scrape gently before blotting.

Deep cleaning: Professional cleaning recommended every 12–18 months. Do not machine wash handmade or wool rugs unless specifically labeled as machine washable.

4.5 — How do I make a warranty claim?

  1. Gather your information: Order number, proof of purchase, and clear photos showing the defect.
  2. Contact our support team via phone, live chat, or email (support@abcretail.com).
  3. Describe the issue clearly — include when it was first noticed and how the item has been used.

Our warranty team will review your claim within 2–3 business days. Resolution options include repair, replacement, or partial/full refund based on the nature of the defect.

Warranty claims submitted more than 30 days after the defect is first noticed may not be eligible for full coverage.

Category 5 — Payment & Financing

5.1 — What payment methods do you accept?

Credit & Debit Cards: Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover

Digital Wallets: PayPal, Apple Pay, Google Pay, Shop Pay

Buy Now, Pay Later: Affirm (0% APR options available on qualifying orders), Klarna (Pay in 4 installments, interest-free)

ABC Retail Gift Cards: Physical and digital gift cards accepted online and in-store

ABC Retail Store Credit: Issued for returns and applied at checkout

We do not currently accept checks, money orders, or wire transfers.

5.2 — Do you offer financing options?

Affirm: Available on orders of $200 or more. 3, 6, or 12-month payment plans. 0% APR available on select promotions; standard APR varies based on creditworthiness. Apply at checkout.

Klarna Pay in 4: Split your purchase into 4 equal payments. First payment at checkout; remaining 3 payments every 2 weeks. No interest or fees when paid on time. Available on orders of $35 to $2,000.

Financing eligibility is subject to credit approval. ABC Retail does not have access to your financing application data — all decisions are made by Affirm or Klarna.

5.3 — When will my card be charged?

Your payment method is charged at the time your order is placed and confirmed.

Exceptions:

  • Made-to-order items: A 50% deposit is charged at the time of order. The remaining 50% is charged when your item ships.
  • Pre-order items: You will not be charged until the item ships.
  • Affirm/Klarna: Payment is structured per your financing agreement.

Temporary authorization holds release automatically within 3–5 business days if the charge does not go through.

5.4 — How do ABC Retail gift cards work?

Purchasing: Available online in denominations of $25, $50, $100, $200, and $500. Physical cards available in-store. Digital cards delivered by email within minutes.

Redeeming: Enter your gift card code at checkout. Multiple gift cards can be applied to a single order and combined with other payment methods.

Terms: Gift cards do not expire. They are not reloadable. Lost or stolen gift cards cannot be replaced without the original purchase receipt. Gift cards cannot be exchanged for cash.
